This is Digworths

Digworths is specialized in UI/UX, Web Design, Software design, Branding and Digital Marketng. Our Work Process

Meeting & Listening

First, we will have a meeting and listen to your needs, what you want to build, and what you want from us

Design

We will take your ideas, prepare the wireframes, mockups, logos, banners and all the visual materials needed

Validation

Next phase is to get your validation. When all is cool, you will approve the visual identity, concept, styles, colors

Build

And here we start the development process, in a SCRUM environment with frequent updates on the progress from our side

Lunch

We will take care of the whole technical launch, prepare the environment so that you enjoy the launch as easy as a Facebook like

Maintain

If you want us to, we can continue our journey by providing tech maintenance and design updates whenever you need

Creativity and Raw talent

We have a pool of highly skilled, constantly improving Software Engineers and Graphic designers that uses the best technologies, tools and softwares to bring your idea to life. Our core stacks are Javascript, React.js, Angular.js, Python, Ruby, PHP, Android, Java, DevOps, iOS, Kotlin, C#, Swift
